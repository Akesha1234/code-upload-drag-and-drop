Preparation Guidelines

  1. Send in your bag of dirty laundry, towels, sheets, etc.
  2. We check all items to make sure they are washable and dryable.
  3. We sort everything by colors, to prevent dye transfer in the wash.
  4. Items are folded immediately; like items are packaged together.
